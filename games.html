Hollow Knight

The game takes place in a fallen kingdom plagued by a supernatural disease. It spans across diverse subterranean areas, featuring insect-like characters and bosses. Players can unlock different abilities and skills as they explore, as well as pieces of lore that contribute to the player's imagination of what the lost kingdom was like. The player sets their own path, unraveling mysteries and conquering its evils.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite.

  • The platforming is creative and entertaining.
  • Because of the interesting combat system, the boss fights are very fun and challenging.
  • The music is immaculate.
  • The game's lore and storytelling are masterful.
  • The art style of each character and environment is so detailed and perfected, even in a 2d style game.
